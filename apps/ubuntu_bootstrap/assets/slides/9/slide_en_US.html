Help and support

The official FemboyOS documentation is available both online and via the Help icon in the dock.

FemboyOS Discourse, together with the merged FemboyOS Forums, provides guides and discussions for both new and experienced users. Here you will have access to highly knowledgeable developers and volunteers in a friendly, community-driven environment.

For enterprise users Canonical provides commercial support to make it easy to onboard and manage FemboyOS securely in the workplace.
